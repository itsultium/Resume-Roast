# Résumé Roast

> Brutal, honest AI feedback on your resume.

Upload or paste your resume, pick your target role, and get specific feedback on exactly what's holding it back — scored across clarity, impact, keywords, formatting, and relevance. Includes a rewritten version of your weakest section.

## Features

- Upload PDF or image (PNG, JPG, WEBP) or paste as text
- Target a specific job title and company for sharper feedback
- Scores across 5 categories: Clarity, Impact, Keywords, Formatting, Relevance
- Detailed breakdown of what's working and what's killing it
- AI-rewritten version of your weakest section
- 3 feedback styles: Brutal, Balanced, Encouraging
- Supports 6 AI providers — bring your own key

## Supported AI Providers

- Anthropic (Claude)
- OpenAI (GPT-4o)
- Google (Gemini)
- Mistral
- Cohere
- xAI (Grok)

## No backend

100% client-side. Your API key is saved in your browser only. No data is stored or sent anywhere except directly to your chosen AI provider.

## Run locally

Open `index.html` in your browser. No build step needed.

## Deploy

Works with GitHub Pages, Vercel, or Netlify.
